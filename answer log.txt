Question:
{Why is hybrid search better than vector-only search?}

Answer:
Hybrid search is better than vector-only search because it combines the strengths of both semantic and keyword-based retrieval:

Vector search provides semantic understanding of the query.
BM25 (keyword search) identifies exact word matches and statistical relevance.

When used together, they complement each other and improve results:

Hybrid search increases accuracy by more than 10% compared to vector-only search.
Adding reranking on top of hybrid search can further improve performance up to 37.2%.

In contrast, relying only on vector search misses exact keyword matches and leads to less optimal retrieval quality.
